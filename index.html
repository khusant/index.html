Tulsa's New Year's Eve Bash is back for the 22nd year of fun and excitement. Be sure to arrive early for all of the festivities and the final countdown. The party starts on December 31 at noon with the FrostiRun 5K and 10K races. From 10 to 11 p.m. we say goodbye to the old year (and ring in the new) with the famous New Year's Eve Bash fireworks spectacular.
